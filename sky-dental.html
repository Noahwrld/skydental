Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Sky Dental different from other clinics? ▼

Sky Dental combines luxury hospitality with world-class dental expertise. Our state-of-the-art technology, internationally trained specialists, and personalized approach ensure an experience unlike any other dental clinic in Kuwait.

How do I book an appointment? ▼

You can book online through our website's booking system, call us at +965 2222 0000, or visit us at our clinic in Kuwait City. Our online booking system allows you to choose your preferred date and time easily.

Do you accept insurance? ▼

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers in Kuwait. Contact our front desk with your insurance details and we'll verify your coverage before your appointment.

Is teeth whitening safe? ▼

Absolutely. Our professional whitening treatments are supervised by experienced dentists and use clinically proven methods. We customize the treatment intensity based on your enamel health to ensure safe, stunning results.

What are your operating hours? ▼

We are open Saturday to Wednesday from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Thursday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We are closed on Fridays. Emergency dental services are available by calling our hotline.

Our Treatments

Comprehensive dental care with a focus on aesthetics and comfort.

✦

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin, custom-crafted porcelain shells that cover imperfections and create a flawless, natural-looking smile. Each veneer is handcrafted by our master ceramists.

◇

Professional Whitening

In-office and take-home whitening options that safely brighten your teeth by up to 8 shades. We use advanced LED-accelerated technology for fast, lasting results.

⬡

Dental Implants

Premium titanium implants with guided surgery for precise placement. Our 3D planning ensures optimal results with minimal discomfort and faster healing.

◎

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Virtually invisible orthodontic treatment for straightening teeth. Custom aligners are replaced every 2 weeks, gradually shifting your teeth into perfect alignment.

◈

Dental Crowns & Bridges

Same-day crowns using CEREC technology and high-quality zirconia or porcelain. Restore damaged teeth with crowns that look and feel completely natural.

❖

Smile Makeover

A comprehensive combination of treatments tailored to completely transform your smile. Includes digital smile design preview so you see the result before treatment begins.
