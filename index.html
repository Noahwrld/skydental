Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect at my first consultation? +

Your first visit includes a comprehensive oral exam, 3D digital imaging, and a personalized consultation with one of our specialists to discuss your aesthetic goals.

Do you offer financing options? +

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans through our partners to ensure your dream smile is accessible. Contact our front desk for details.

How long does the veneer process take? +

Typically, the process takes 2-3 visits over a span of 2-3 weeks, including design, preparation, and final placement.
